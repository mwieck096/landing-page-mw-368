The Grief Collection

The Grief Collection is a haunting, lyrical exploration of what remains after love is lost, faith is questioned, and memory becomes both refuge and ruin. Woven through intimate reflections and vivid fragments, these poems journey through the many chambers of grief—its tenderness, its fury, and its quiet persistence. From the echoes of childhood to the scars left by silence, from whispered prayers to the voices of women breaking through generations of restraint, this collection contemplates what it means to mourn and to remember. Rooted in personal experience and rich with symbolism, the poems give shape to pain while honoring the complex beauty that often lives beneath it. At once deeply personal and fiercely resonant, The Grief Collection invites readers to sit with sorrow, speak with ghosts, and find a strange kind of grace in the shadows.
